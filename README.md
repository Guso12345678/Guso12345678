## About me
 
I'm a Mathematical Engineering and Artificial Intelligence student at Universidad Pontificia Comillas (ICAI), passionate about building things that sit at the intersection of mathematics, software and real hardware. My projects range from low-level GPU kernel implementations to autonomous robots, full-stack web apps and quantum machine learning.
 
I enjoy working across the full stack — from soldering sensors on a Raspberry Pi to training diffusion models from scratch in PyTorch.
